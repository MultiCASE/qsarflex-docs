# Product Overview

**Advanced Web-Based (Q)SAR & Toxicological Prediction Platform by MultiCASE**

---

# 🔍 Overview

 brings the full power of MultiCASE’s cutting-edge QSAR and Read-Across modeling tools to your browser—no installation needed. Designed for regulatory, pharmaceutical, and chemical safety professionals, QF-Web enables rapid, evidence-based toxicity prediction and compound analysis.

> “All the goodness of MultiCASE models — now in the browser.”

---

# 💡 Key Features

- **Predictive Modeling**
  - Structure-based (Q)SAR and Read-Across models
  - Descriptor calculations and evidence-based predictions
  - Supports N-nitrosamines, impurities (e.g., NDSRIs), and more
- **Flexible Applications**
  - Use in pharmaceuticals, environmental risk assessment, cosmetics, and chemical manufacturing
- **User-Friendly Interface**
  - Load compounds via SMILES, InChI, or registry number
  - Visualize molecules and reactions instantly
  - Run batch or single evaluations
- **Modular Analysis Engine**
  - Select from licensed modules (e.g., Soil Adsorption, Water Solubility, Tetrahymena GC50, N-Nitroso)
  - Generate detailed reports with one click
